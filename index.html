About Me

Most project managers spend hours every week chasing down updates from manufacturers, sifting through emails, and sitting in meetings to piece together a project's status. I solve this by implementing a custom interface that connects your team directly with the manufacturer's production data. This system eliminates the need to chase people for updates, giving your project managers a single, real-time dashboard to see exactly what's happening on the shop floor, so they can focus on what matters most: keeping the project on track.
